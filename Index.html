About Us

Built on Empathy & Transparency

Zeeshan Shafqat Founder| Social Worker & Tech Enthusiast

Mir Welfare Trust is a Non-Governmental Organization committed to empowering underprivileged communities. We focus on essential services: ration distribution, education, clean water, and healthcare. Our mission is to uplift lives with dignity, respect, and compassion.

Water Filter Installation Drive Has Begun

Thanks to your generous support, Mir Welfare Trust has successfully installed water filtration unit in underserved rural area of sindh. These filters provide families with clean, safe drinking water reducing waterborne diseases and giving children a healthier start in life.
